The objective of this game is to reach a five star rating for your tower.

You are the proud owner of a no-star tower that consists merely of an empty floor with no units. However you have just received a small inheritance of ${{$ctrl.game.tower.funds}} from your long-lost, twice-removed, late cousin's granny all the way from Timbuktu. Build as fast as possible for a better score!

Each floor of your tower supports 6 spaces to add units.
